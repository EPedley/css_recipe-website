INGREDIENTS
  1. 500g lean lamb mince
  2. 2 small preserved lemons
  3. 10 pitted green olives, finely chopped
  4. Small bunch of parsley
  5. 1.5 tbsp ras-el-hanout
  6. 1 tbsp vegetable oil
Lamb koftas
A couple of the ingredients for this recipe are a bit hard to find, but available at most Turkish and Greek supermarkets. These koftas are best cooked over a grill or barbecue and served with copious amounts of yoghurt, soft, warm pitta and a tomato and onion salad.
  1. In a large bowl, combine the lamb, lemons, parsley, olives and ras-el-hanout.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 and shape each piece around a long skewer.
  3. Chill for 30 mins.
  4. Heat the oil on a grill.
  5. Wrap tin foil around the base of the skewers.
  6. Place on a baking tray and grill for 7 mins on each side until charred.
