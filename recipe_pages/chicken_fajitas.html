INGREDIENTS
  1. 700g chicken breast
  2. 7 arbol chillis
  3. 3 guajillo chillis
  4. 1 pasilli chilli
  5. 1 ancho chilli
  6. 1/2 lime
  7. 1 tsp Slap Yo Mama seasoning
  8. 3 tbsp oil
  9. 1/2 small red onion
  10. 6 garlic cloves
  11. 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  12. 1/2 tsp cumin
  13. 2 tsp salt
  14. 2 tsp smoked paprika
